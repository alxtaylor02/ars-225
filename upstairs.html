UPSTAIRS IN THE CREEP HOUSE...

 

The stairs crack and croak as the four teens make their way upstairs. Gabby notices the rectangular shadows on the walls where pictures used to be. Those spots are the only places to show the true color of the wallpaper: a deep green with intricate swirls embossed on it.

 

As they reached the top of the staircase, the hallway branched out towards the left. The creaky floor turned to carpet
